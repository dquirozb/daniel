Welcome. I am an Associate Professor (Profesor Adjunto) at the Institute of Mathematical Engineering at the Universidad de Valparaíso, Chile.

Before reaching Valparaíso I was a Visiting Assistant Professor at the Department of Mathematical Engineering at Universidad de Chile. Before that I held a post-doc position Center for Mathematical Modeling, working with Maya Stein. I completed my PhD at the Department of Mathematics of the London School of Economics, under the supervision of Jan van den Heuvel.

My research interest lie in combinatorics, particularly graph coloring and structural graph theory.

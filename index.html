About

Hello. I am a Visiting Assistant Professor (Profesor Adjunto) at the Department of Mathematical Engineering at the Universidad de Chile.

I am also an associate researcher at the Center for Mathematical Modeling, where I held a one-year (Sept. 2017-Sept. 2018) post-doc position, working with Maya Stein. I completed my PhD at the Department of Mathematics of the London School of Economics, under the supervision of Jan van den Heuvel.

My research interest lie in combinatorics, particularly in the interplay between chromatic and structural properties of graphs.

For further information, you can look at the Research and Teaching sections of this website

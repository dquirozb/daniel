CODICIS

- Colors, dimension and covers in graphs -


Welcome to the webpage of this joint scientific project of universities from Brazil, Chile and France (2024-2025).
The project is funded by ANID (Chile), CAPES (Brazil), and MEAE (France) under the collaboration program MATH-AMSUD (grant number: 23-MATH-02).

The main topics of the project are the following:

  • Topic 1: The immersion analogue of Hadwiger's conjecture.
  • Topic 2: Chi-boundedness of graphs with excluded subgraphs.
  • Topic 3: Metric dimension of graphs.
  • Topic 4: Decompositions and covers of edge-coloured graphs.
